## 4. 实际应用

### 4.1 监控实现

**监控查询**：

```sql
-- 监控指标
SELECT
    workflow_id,
    AVG(execution_time) AS avg_time,
    COUNT(*) FILTER (WHERE status = 'success') / COUNT(*)::DOUBLE AS success_rate
FROM workflow_executions
GROUP BY workflow_id;
```
